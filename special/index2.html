JAPANESE BUFFET




Our Japanese Dinner Buffet is held every last week of the month. As a Japanese-owned hotel, we are committed to providing our guests with an exceptional dining experience that is true to Japanese traditions and flavors.

Our Executive Chef, a native of Japan, curates the menu to ensure that every dish is authentic and delicious. From sushi and sashimi to tempura and teriyaki, our buffet offers a wide variety of dishes that will satisfy your cravings and delight your taste buds.

Join us for an evening of gastronomic delights, where you can indulge in unlimited servings of our carefully crafted Japanese dishes. Our restaurant's elegant ambiance provides the perfect setting for a memorable dining experience with family, friends, or colleagues.

Reservations are highly recommended for our Japanese Dinner Buffet, so be sure to contact our team through social media or our hotline to secure your spot.
