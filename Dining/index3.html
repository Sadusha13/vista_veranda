The Bar on the Top

Towering over 100 meters off the glistening waters of the Indian Ocean and the busy city, our Rooftop Bar provides the perfect escape, with a refreshing side of thirst quenchers. From tropical fruit juices to a colourful range of cocktails carefully concocted by our in-house mixologists, grab the best seat and get ready to unwind. Our range of sundowners are exceptionally perfect while watching a spectacular sunset. Don’t miss it!!
